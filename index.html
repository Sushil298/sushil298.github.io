Research

My research interest lies at the intersection of robotics, security, and machine learning, aiming to enhance both safety and functionality in autonomous systems.

Graph Neural Network (GNN) for Hardware Security
Center for Cyber Security, NYU Abu Dhabi
May 2023 - Present

GNN is leveraged to analyze digital circuits to determine the presence of any hardware trojans using a binary classification algorithm. The process is executed directly on the hardware via Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs) and Verilog.

Large Language Model Cybersecurity for Hardware Design
Center for Cyber Security, NYU New York
June 2022 - August 2022

Machine learning model is trained to generate the Verilog codes based on the user's prompt.

Four Link Anthropomorphic Robotic Arm with Gripper
November 2022

Link Video

This is a dexterous robotic arm with a gripper. It simulates using Simulink and utilizes MATLAB for kinematics. Physical parts are 3D printed and Herkulex smart servo motors are used for joints. Unreachable destinations maintain the initial position and orientation.

PCB - Heart Rate Monitor
November 2022

Link

It captures the QRS waveform of ECG signal using electrodes and connects to an oscilloscope. It incorporates a right LEG circuit, an instrumentation amplifier, an active band-pass filter, and decoupling capacitors. The PCB layout was prepared with Altium for manufacturing.
